ABOUT

I’m Aidin, a Cape Town–based songwriter, producer, mixing engineer, and mastering engineer, born in 2003. Music has always been my language — whether I’m writing, recording, or shaping a record from demo to final master.

I work across the full creative process, combining strong songwriting with technical precision to deliver polished, industry-ready music. My approach is artist-first: every decision serves the emotion, identity, and impact of the song.

From intimate vocals to fully produced records, I focus on creating music that connects, translates, and lasts.
