ABOUT ME

I’m a songwriter, producer, mixing engineer, and mastering engineer. Music is my life, whether I’m performing, writing, recording, or shaping a record from demo to final master.

I'm able to work across the full creative process, combining songwriting with technical precision to deliver polished, industry-ready music. My approach is artist-first: every decision made focuses on emotion, identity, and impact of the song.

From intimate vocals to fully produced records, I focus on creating music that connects, translates, and lasts.
